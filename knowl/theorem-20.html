Theorem 2.1.7.

Let \(A\) be \(n\times n\text{,}\) with \(n\geq 2\text{,}\) and let \(\tilde{A}\) be the matrix obtained by swapping two rows of \(A\text{.}\) Then \(\det(A)=-\det(\tilde{A})\text{.}\)

The same is true if \(\tilde{A}\) is the result of swapping two columns of \(A\text{.}\)
