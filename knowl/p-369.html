The expansions for \(\det(A)\) and \(\det(\tilde{A})\) differ only in terms of their minors, \(M_{k\ell}\) and \(\tilde{M}_{k\ell}\text{,}\) and these differ only in that the square matrices of size \(n-1\) involved have two rows swapped: the ones corresponding the \(i\)-th and \(j\)-th rows of \(A\text{!}\)
