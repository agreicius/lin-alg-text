Given an \(m\times n\) matrix \(A=[a_{ij}]\) its transpose \(A^T\) is the matrix whose \(ij\)-entry is the \(ji\)-th entry of \(A\text{.}\) Using notation we have \(A^T=[b_{ij}]_{n\times m}\text{,}\) where \(b_{ij}=a_{ji}\text{.}\) Note in particular that \(A^T\) is \(n\times m\text{.}\)
