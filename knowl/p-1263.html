The orthogonal projection \(\boldw=\proj{\boldv}{W}\) is the element of \(W\) \alert{closest} to \(\boldv\text{.}\) This means \(\norm{\boldv-\proj{\boldv}{W}}\leq\norm{\boldv-\boldw'}\) for any other \(\boldw'\in W\text{.}\) We thus define the distance from \(\boldv\) to \(W\) as \(d(\boldv, W)=\norm{\boldv-\proj{\boldv}{W}}=\norm{\boldw^\perp}\text{.}\)
