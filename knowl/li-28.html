Item 1.

Divide the variables \(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_5\) into leading variables and free variables: a leading variable is one corresponding to a column of the row reduced matrix that contains a leading 1; a free variable is any variable that is not a leading one. In our case, \(x_1, x_3\text{,}\) and \(x_5\) are leading, and the rest, \(x_2, x_4\) are free.
