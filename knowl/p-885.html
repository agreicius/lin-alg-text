As the notation indicates, both of these operations depend essentially on your choice of basis or bases. This gives rise to the following questions:

  1. Given \(V\) and two choices of basis, \(B\) and \(B'\text{,}\) what is the relation between \([\boldv]_B\) and \([\boldv]_{B'}\text{?}\)

  2. GIven \(T\colon V\rightarrow W\) and two choices of pairs of bases, \((B, B')\) and \((B'', B''')\text{,}\) what is the relation between \([T]_{B}^{B'}\) and \([T]_{B''}^{B'''}\text{?}\)
