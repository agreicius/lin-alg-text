Definition 2.1.2.

Let \(A\) be \(n\times n\text{.}\)

The \(ij\)-th minor of \(A\), denoted \(M_{ij}\text{,}\) is the determinant of the submatrix of \(A\) obtained by deleting the \(i\)-th row and \(j\)-th column.

The \(ij\)-th cofactor of \(A\), denoted \(C_{ij}\text{,}\) is defined as

\begin{equation*} C_{ij}=(-1)^{i+j}M_{ij}\text{.} \end{equation*}
