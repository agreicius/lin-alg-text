Corollary 4.4.2. Computational method.

Any question about the linear transformation \(T\) can be answered by first answering the analagous question for the matrix \(A=[T]_B^{B'}\text{,}\) and then translating your results back to \(V\) and \(W\) using \([\hspace{5pt}]_B^{-1}\) and \([\hspace{5pt}]_{B'}^{-1}\text{.}\)
