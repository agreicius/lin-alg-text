Given a matrix \(B_{n\times r}\text{,}\) we will think of \(B\) as a sequence of \(m\) row vectors:

\begin{equation*} \begin{bmatrix}b_{11}\amp b_{12}\amp \cdots \amp b_{1r}\\ \hline b_{21}\amp b_{22}\amp \cdots \amp b_{2r}\\ \hline \vdots\amp \vdots \amp \cdots\amp \vdots \\ \hline b_{n1}\amp b_{n2}\amp \cdots\amp b_{nr} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}-\boldb_1-\\ -\boldb_2- \\ \vdots \\ -\boldb_n- \end{bmatrix} \end{equation*}
