Definition 1.3.3.

Suppose \(A=[a_{ij}]\) and \(B=[b_{ij}]\) are both \(m\times n\) matrices. We define

  • their sum \(A+B\) to be the matrix \(C=[a_{ij}+b_{ij}]\text{;}\)

  • their difference \(A-B\) to be the matrix \(D=[a_{ij}-b_{ij}]\text{.}\)

Equivalently, we \(A+B\) and \(A-B\) are defined by declaring:

\begin{align*} (A+B)_{ij}\amp =\amp (A)_{ij}+(B)_{ij}\\ (A-B)_{ij}\amp =\amp (A)_{ij}-B_{ij} \end{align*}
