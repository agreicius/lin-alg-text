Definition 1.4.2.

Two matrices \(A\) and \(B\) are equal when

  1. they have the same size, and

  2. their corresponding entries are equal.

In other words, \(A\) and \(B\) must both be \(m\times n\) matrices, and \((A)_{ij}=(B)_{ij}\) for all \(1\leq i\leq m\) and \(1\leq j\leq n\text{.}\)
