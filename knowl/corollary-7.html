Corollary 2.2.4.

Let \(A\) be \(n\times n\text{,}\) and denote the rows of \(A\) by \(\boldr_i\text{.}\) If \(\boldr_i=c\boldr_j\) for some \(i\ne j\) and some scalar \(c\text{,}\) then \(\det(A)=0\text{.}\)

Similarly if \(A\) has two columns that are scalar multiples of one another, then \(\det(A)=0\text{.}\)
