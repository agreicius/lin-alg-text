Theorem 4.5.1. Change of basis for transformations.

Let \(V\) be finite-dimensional, let \(T\colon V\rightarrow V\) be linear, and let \(B\) and \(B'\) be two bases for \(V\text{.}\)

Then

\begin{align*} _{B'}\amp =\underset{B\rightarrow B'}{P}[T]_B\underset{B'\rightarrow B}{P}\\ \amp =\left( \underset{B'\rightarrow B}{P}\right)^{-1}[T]_B\underset{B'\rightarrow B}{P} \end{align*}
