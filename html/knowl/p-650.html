GE shows that the system has a solution for any choice of \(a_2, a_1, a_0\text{:}\) namely, \(r=-a_2+a_1+a_0\text{,}\) \(s=a_2-a_0\text{,}\) \(t=a_2-a_1\text{.}\) Thus given any \(p(x)=a_2x^2+a_1x+a_0\text{,}\) we can find \(r,s,t\) such that \(p=rp_1+sp_2+tp_3\text{,}\) showing \(P_2\subseteq \Span(S)\text{.}\)
